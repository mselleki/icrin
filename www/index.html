ABOUT US

The thrust of our research initiative revolves around two major themes:

  1. The Brain Stimulation lab endeavors to understand the neural basis of obsessive compulsive disorder, unipolar depression and substance use disorders employing cutting-edge computational techniques such as machine learning algorithms.
  2. We can now also offer treatment resistant patients innovative therapeutics such as Deep Brain Stimulation and repetitive Magnetic Transcranial Stimulation in order to give them the benefit of an emerging new care process to help improve their quality of life.
