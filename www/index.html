ABOUT US

The thrust of our research initiative revolves around two major themes:

  1. The ICRIN lab endeavors to understand the neural basis of post-traumatic stress disorder, substance use disorders and obsessive-compulsive disorder, employing cutting-edge computational techniques such as machine learning algorithms applied to neuroimaging and EEG data.

  2. We can now also offer treatment resistant patients innovative non-invasive (transcranial direct current stimulation, repetitive magnetic transcranial stimulation and deep transcranial magnetic stimulation) and invasive (deep brain stimulation) in order to give them the benefit of an emerging new care process to help improve their quality of life.
